Classic Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Description

Dive into the ultimate comfort food with this Classic Grilled Cheese Sandwich recipe. It's a simple yet delicious dish that pairs perfectly with a bowl of tomato soup or serves as a satisfying meal on its own. With just a few ingredients and a couple of minutes, you'll have a golden, crispy sandwich with a melty cheese center.

Ingredients

  • 4 slices of bread (white or whole wheat)
  • 4 slices of cheese (Cheddar, American, or your choice)
  • 2 tablespoons butter, softened

Steps

  1. Preheat the Skillet: Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat.
  2. Butter the Bread: Spread butter evenly on one side of each bread slice. The buttered side will be the outer part of the sandwich to get that golden, crispy texture.
  3. Assemble the Sandwich: Place two slices of bread, buttered side down, on the skillet. Add two slices of cheese on each, then top with the remaining bread slices, buttered side up.
  4. Grill the Sandwich: Cook for about 3-4 minutes on the first side until the bread is golden brown. Carefully flip the sandwich over and press down slightly with a spatula. Cook for another 3-4 minutes until the second side is golden brown and the cheese has melted.
  5. Serve Immediately: Remove from the skillet, slice in half if desired, and serve hot.
